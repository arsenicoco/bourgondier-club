Wine Tasting Club "Bourgondier Weekly"

The word "bourgondier" in Dutch means a lover of life. We decided that there's no better name for a weekly wine lovers club.

We're gathering a club of like-minded people interested in wine and everything related to it. This is not a professional course, neither a master class, and certainly not wine education. It's an opportunity for regular networking and knowledge sharing: how to choose wine, how to understand it, where to buy and store it, and how to enjoy it to the maximum.

Our meetings take place every Friday in Amsterdam Center, from 6:30 PM to 8:00 PM. We'll try 5-7 wines, selected for the evening's theme.
